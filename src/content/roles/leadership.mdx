## Key Questions Answered

<Collapsible variant="tip" title="What's our AI risk exposure?">

The [Intake Quiz](/intake/project-intake) classifies every project by risk level. Green and Yellow projects are low-risk internal tools that need minimal oversight. Orange and Red projects require formal review before launch. See [Security Standards](/standards/security) for the specific controls applied at each level.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible variant="tip" title="How much should we budget for AI tools?">

See [Tooling Strategy](/guides/tool-selection) for cost planning frameworks and the [Approved Tools](/client-config/approved-tools) list for current spend. Most Green-path tools cost under £50/month. Yellow and above may involve platform subscriptions or API costs that need line-item budgeting.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible variant="tip" title="Are we compliant for student-facing AI?">

See [Safeguarding & Under-18s](/standards/safeguarding) for our obligations under UK GDPR, DPA 2018, and the ICO Age Appropriate Design Code. All student-facing AI requires Yellow path minimum, which includes additional review, data handling controls, and safeguarding checks before launch.

</Collapsible>
