# Vibe Coding Framework

**Build AI-powered tools — quickly, safely, and consistently.**

VCF guides you through every step of building with AI coding tools
so you don't need to be a developer or memorize every rule.
The framework handles that for you.

---

## 🚀 How it works

<StepCard number={1} title="Tell us what you're building">
  Answer 5 quick questions about your project. The framework
  picks the right level of guidance.
  [Start the intake →](/intake/project-intake)
</StepCard>

<StepCard number={2} title="Get your build path">
  Your answers map to one of four paths: Quick Build, Standard Build,
  Reviewed Build, or Protected Build.
</StepCard>

<StepCard number={3} title="Follow the guide">
  Step-by-step instructions while you build.
  The guide teaches you as you go.
  [See build guides →](/build-guides/green-quick-build)
</StepCard>

<StepCard number={4} title="Run the checklist">
  Quick final check before going live.
  Confirm everything's in order.
  [View checklists →](/checklists/green-checklist)
</StepCard>

---

<Admonition type="info" title="🧭 First time here?">

<Admonition type="tip" title="💬 Stuck or unsure?">
